Black Box Investigation

ESUMS

Claim

The mystery black box is a box with mystery functions. Something inside the black box makes it so that if you pour a certain amount of water you can get either less or more water. The black box was built by Mr. Sinusas for New Media and Tech for Social Change. There is not enough evidence to make a claim about what is in the black box.

Evidence and Reasoning

In Mr.Sinusas's version of the black box there were many variations of data.Each input of water had a different amount in output. For example on trial #19, 1000mL of water was poured in and we were given 1730mL. We measured the amount of water before and after we poured it in. The bottom of the box also had some leakage indicating that something in the box was either overflowing or had a hole. The leakage could also be due to how old the box is and it could have been worn down over the years.

In my team's version of the black box we were not successful in accurately replicating what was in the original version. No matter how many trails we did we got no amount of water coming out. We also had a lot of leaking from the siphon and where the two main pipes were connected. For example in trial 12 we poured in an unknown amount of water, received nothing, and major leakage occurred. Other teams had similar problems, yet their box gave some output. The box we made was a pretty simple design but did not turn out how we wanted it to. Our box also had many flaws which can be taken into account. With this said I know for a fact that what my team built is NOT in the original design.

Conclusion

In conclusion there is no way to confirm what is or is not in the black box without seeing it. Another way would be to test a different design more simpler than the one my team made to see if that is closer to the black box Mr.Sinusas made.
